## Experience

### NYC Office of Technology & Innovation (OTI) - Office of Data & Analytics (ODA)
**Director of Data Governance** | Feb 2024 - Present

- Delivered NYC Agency and Governance Organizations (303 entities), the City's first open, standardized agency reference, democratizing access to government structure by consolidating 8 fragmented lists into a public resource now powering the NYC.gov Agency Directory.
- Co-led MetaCat development: internal metadata catalog improving discoverability and usability of non-public datasets, with 325+ data assets from 21 agencies.
- Trained 400+ city staff across 15 agencies on WorkerConnect, NYC's cross-agency data-sharing platform enabling caseworkers to better serve vulnerable New Yorkers.
- Built open-source tools enabling LLMs to query NYC Open Data APIs; delivered 4 presentations on responsible AI use for government data through the Open Data Ambassador program.
- Drove data standards working groups including the Data Asset Working Group; created internal collaboration website (nycdata.nycnet) consolidating 35 resources for the initiative.

### NYC Department of Homeless Services
**Executive Director of Data & Analytics; Founding Director, Street Homeless Solutions Lab** | 2018 - Feb 2024

- Founded Street Homeless Solutions Lab, a 3-person product and service design team; secured funding and executive sponsorship to center user needs in technology decisions.
- Designed and implemented participatory policy co-creation using Polis and vTaiwan-inspired methods, engaging 300 frontline and executive staff across 17 programs in consensus-driven practice redesign - among the first vTaiwan-inspired processes in U.S. city government.
- Directed cross-system data integration for street homeless clients, including racial equity analysis exposing disparities in placements; produced evidence that secured 700-800 additional supportive housing units.
